Deep Fakes

Deep fakes use a form of artificial intelligence called deep learning which substitutes a person's likeness with another. Deep fakes are not only utilised in a video format, but also can be used to generate voice cloning. To create a deep fake video, you must first run thousands of images through an AI algorithm encoder. The encoder learns the features and compresses them into images for a decoder to then recover them. Two decoders are used for each face, and to achieve the deep fake, one feeds the encoded images into the wrong decoder. The faces are reconstructed from each orientation on every frame.
