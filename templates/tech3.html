Quantum Computing

Quantum computing uses the physics of subatomic particles to perform calculations and simulations too complex for a standard computer to process. Quantum computers are not limited to binary bits, but instead utilise quantum bits that can represent a one, zero, or both. These computers approach problems simultaneously, rather than sequentially. This vastly increses the computational speed of these computers.
